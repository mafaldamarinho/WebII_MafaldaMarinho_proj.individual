TRÊS FICÇÕES

This project consists of the page layout and graphic editing of the book, Three Fictions of Jorge Luis Borges. The format of this (20x14) has been chosen so as to provide a reading mode that is most comfortable for the player. The cover is a paperback with flaps, which contains a synopsis of the book and a biography of the author. This follows a simple concept, related to the division of the book into three chapters and your plot associated with multiple mazes of words, this contains a composition that may go against these aspects, have represented the title of the book, almost like a maze,also the reading that is not immediate, in the sense that it makes reference to the confusion and pun intended the same language.
